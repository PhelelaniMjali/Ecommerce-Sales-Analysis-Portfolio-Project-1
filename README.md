# Ecommerce-Sales-Analysis-Portfolio-Project-1

This report presents an analysis of the ecommerce sales dataset. The objective of the project was to clean, organize, and visualize the sales data to identify business trends, performance indicators, and actionable recommendations.

## Data Summary

The dataset contains sales transaction records across multiple product categories, regions, and years. The analysis focused on sales performance, profitability, product demand, and regional trends.

### Total Records Analyzed

* 3,500 sales records

### Overall Performance

* **Total Sales:** 10,667,881
* **Total Profit:** 1,844,665.21


# Key Insights

* Electronics was the highest-performing category, generating the largest share of total sales revenue.
* The West region recorded the highest overall sales performance compared to other regions.
* Camera products achieved the highest individual product sales among all products in the dataset.
* Sales increased significantly from 2022 to 2023, showing strong business growth trends.
* Profitability remained positive across the dataset, indicating effective pricing and operational performance.
* Seasonal sales variations were observed across different months, highlighting periods of higher customer demand.

# Recommendations

* Increase inventory and marketing investment in high-performing categories such as Electronics to maximize revenue growth.
* Focus promotional campaigns in top-performing regions while developing strategies to improve lower-performing regions.
* Expand product offerings related to top-selling products like Cameras and other electronics accessories.
* Use monthly sales trends to plan seasonal promotions and optimize stock management.
* Continue monitoring profit margins to ensure sustainable business growth and operational efficiency.

# Tools Used

* Python for cleaning the data
* Power BI for visualization


# Conclusion

The analysis revealed strong sales performance in the Electronics category and significant regional differences in customer demand. The insights obtained from the dataset can support better business decisions related to marketing, inventory planning, and profitability management. Using Python and Power BI enabled efficient data preparation and clear visualization of business trends.
